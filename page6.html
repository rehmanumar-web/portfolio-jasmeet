💌 To the Girl of My Dreams

To the girl of my dreams,

You have this way of always being in my thoughts regardless of what I'm doing at all.

Words cannot describe how much I love you, even you probably can't comprehend how much I do. And if I had to choose to relive my life 1000 times or more, I would still look for your name in every lifetime.

I wish to spend the rest of my life with you if you end up choosing me, because I strongly believe that we are meant for each other.

Even through difficult times, no matter how hard things were, you still stuck with me and chose me regardless. I am forever grateful for that.

Now… there's just one more thing I'd like to show you.

And one last thing…

I'd love to make memories with you till my last breath.
